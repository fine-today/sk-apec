2025 APEC CEO SUMMIT,
Future Tech Forum Series: AI

The Future Tech Forum AI Session is a special program held in conjunction with the APEC CEO Summit, bringing together global AI industry leaders, AI scholars, and senior government officials to engage in in-depth discussions on the latest AI technologies, trends, and key issues.

Host Greeting

Chey Tae-won

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to the Future Tech Forum – AI, a special program of the APEC CEO Summit Korea 2025.

This Forum will focus on building Korea’s AI ecosystem and provide a platform where global technology leaders, distinguished scholars, and government officials come together to exchange ideas on the future of AI. By sharing insights and visions, we aim to foster collaboration that bridges local innovation with global opportunities.

Through these discussions, we hope to explore how AI can empower industries, create new value for society, and contribute to sustainable growth across the Asia-Pacific region.

I look forward to your active participation in shaping this meaningful dialogue on AI.

Chey Tae-won Chairman, SK Group
